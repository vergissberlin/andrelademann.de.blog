If one day you get bitten by the bug and start developing, you still have no idea why you got involved. You have a huge amount of work ahead of you that needs to be done. You have to learn a lot, and you will never be able to stop, because there is always something new.

Sounds scary, doesn't it? The colleagues at [roadmap.sh](https://roadmap.sh) will bring some order to your ordeal. The roadmap gives you a good orientation for which technologies you can decide on.

I don't agree with every suggestion, but I agree with most of them. After all, it's not a bad thing to specialise in something others can't do.
