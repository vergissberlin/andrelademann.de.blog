I'm André Lademann — Cloud Architect, Developer, and Tech Lead at [Thinkport](https://thinkport.digital), an IT Cloud Consulting company based in Germany.

I write about cloud architecture, software engineering practices, tooling, and the occasional hardware experiment. Topics range from infrastructure as code and Kubernetes to Flutter, web APIs, and AI integrations.

## Background

I've been building software professionally for over a decade, with a strong focus on scalable, secure, and maintainable cloud-native systems. My day-to-day involves designing cloud architectures, mentoring teams, and shipping production-grade solutions on AWS and Azure.

Outside of work, I enjoy tinkering with microcontrollers (ESP32, Arduino), contributing to open-source projects, and writing about things I find interesting enough to share.
